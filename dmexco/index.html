Road to Cultivating Harmony

Tim Geenen, General Manager, Faktor, a LiveRamp company; speaking on Innovation and Regulation: the Road to Cultivating Harmony

Location: World of Agencies Stage
Date: September 12, 2019
Time: 1:55 - 2:15 pm

It seems like policymakers and the advertising industry are at odds. However, the time is now to remain innovative while staying ahead of data privacy regulations. Faktor’s General Manager Tim Geenen joins Mila Dimitrova, Data Protection Officer of Nova Broadcasting Group, to discuss ways to revamp or enhance products and audit data and marketing strategies to ensure businesses are built on an ethical and sustainable foundation.

Consumer Identity and Privacy

Travis Clinger, VP of Strategic Partnerships; speaking on Consumer Identity and Privacy: Our Industry's Biggest Challenge or Biggest Opportunity?

IAB Tech Lab Seminar at DMEXCO
Location: Cologne Conference Center, Seminar 5
Date: September 12, 2019
Time: 2:00 pm

With so many changes happening, they leave our industry with many unanswered questions: how do we respect consumer privacy choices if we can't recognize them? Will basic addressability in the future only be available in silos by first parties? Is there something we can do as an industry? These questions and more will be covered by IAB Tech Lab's seminar, featuring LiveRamp’s Travis Clinger, VP of Strategic Partnerships, along with other industry leaders.
