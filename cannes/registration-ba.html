Tuesday, June 18

LiveRamp Innovation Roundtables

Le Gray D’Albion Penthouse
Lunch and Discussion: 12:00 pm - 1:30 pm
Cocktail hour: 1:30 pm - 2:30 pm

*We’re currently at capacity for our lunch roundtables. If you’re interested in attending, please indicate when you register and we’ll add you to the waitlist.

A productive business discussion requires input from stakeholders across the industry. Join leaders from agencies, brands, publishers, media, and technology as we address some of the biggest challenges facing the marketing industry today:

Table Topic: Maintaining Omnichannel Addressability with Identity

Today, there’s 300 cookie domains, more in-app spending, and a CTV market that will surpass display in the next year—with no common identifier. What’s more, 30% of all browser traffic has no identity. Come discuss how upgrading the infrastructure of the ecosystem with a neutral people-based identity can drive transparent measurement, drastically improve programmatic spend, and strengthen consumer privacy.

Table Topic: Bridging the TV-Digital Divide

Digital and TV are converging. Leading companies will merge their teams to share best practices, technology, and data. Let’s discuss how you’re tackling this new era.

Table Topic: Planning for the Future of Data Privacy and Consent

With new regulation shaking our industry, what is the best way to build a robust, compliant data strategy? Huddle with partners to take control of your data-driven future.

Table Topic: How Can Data and Technology Improve Storytelling

Your brand has a story to tell. The age-old debate is no longer one. You need to have technologists and creatives working together to have a successful marketing campaign. Let’s discuss how you make room for both on your team and how they work best together to amplify your brand’s story.
